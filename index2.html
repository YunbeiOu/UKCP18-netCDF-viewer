About project

This website was developed by Yunbei Ou in the Master's dissertation projection, under the supervision of Professor Richard Kingston from University of Manchester.

This is the link to all codes: https://github.com/joou1232, reproducible for similar work on visualizing NetCDF data (e.g. UKCP18) in web map.


About data

The climate projections data are downloaded from UKCP18 dataset provided by The CEDA Archive. And 'UKCP18 Local Projection 01' was used to demonstrate.
